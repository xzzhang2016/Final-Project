2013 World Development Indicator (WDI)

World Development Indicators is the primary World Bank collection of development indicators, compiled from officially-recognized international sources. It includes data on over 800 indicators covering more than 150 economies. Some of the indicator categories are agriculture & rural development, aid effectivenss, climate change, economy & growth, and education.

2013 Global Health Observatory (GHO) Data

The GHO data covers health-related statistics for more than 1000 indicators for the 194 Member States of WHO. Data are organized to monitor progress towards the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), including health status indicators to monitor progress towards for the overal health goal, indicators to track equity in health indicators, and the indicators for the specific health and health-related targets of the SDGS.
